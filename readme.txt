DESCRIPTION:

Bright is a clean, modern and well crafted responsive template designed for creatives and agencies. 
The template suits the needs of creative agencies, freelancers, and even small business websites. 
Bright is mobile and retina/hi-dpi ready which means your site will look awesome, crisp and sharp 
on any screen resolutions and devices. Also, the code behind Bright is clean and well organized 
which makes the template very easy to customize.
